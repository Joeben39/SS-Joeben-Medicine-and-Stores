My name is Obiefuna Joseph Ifunanya

I am the founder and managing director of SS Joeben Medicine and stores. SS Joeben Medicine and Stores is a pharmaceutical store that specializes in offering a wide range of genuine and verifiable drugs from various reputable brands. Our products are carefully selected to ensure they meet the highest standards of quality and efficacy. We cater to diverse healthcare needs, providing medications that support overall health and well-being. Our commitment to authenticity and reliability ensures that customers receive safe and effective pharmaceutical solutions tailored to their specific medical requirements. Obiefuna Joseph Ifunanya brings a blend of academic knowledge and practical experience to his role as Managing Director of SS Joeben Medicine and Stores. With a strong educational background in health information management and Industrial chemistry, he possesses a comprehensive understanding of both the scientific and logistical aspects of the pharmaceutical industry. His experience as a patent medicine vendor and in drug wholesale and supply has equipped him with the necessary skills to oversee the store's operations, ensure the quality and authenticity of the products, and drive the business towards achieving its strategic goals. Joseph’s leadership is instrumental in maintaining high standards of customer service and operational efficiency, aligning with the company's mission to facilitate the efficient circulation and supply of quality drugs and medicines. His dedication to the healthcare sector and his vision for SS Joeben Medicine and Stores are key drivers of the store’s success and growth in meeting the healthcare needs of the community.
